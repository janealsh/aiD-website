# aiD website

This is the website for The Norwegian Center on AI for Decision (AID), the aiD Space.

AID is a recent NTNU initiative on Artificial Intelligence (AI) for Decision Making and a proposal for a National AI Center. It is a joint effort of NTNU and SINTEF led by Sebastien Gros (NTNU, Department of Engineering Cybernetics) and Signe Riemen-SÃ¸rensen (SINTEF Digital) and with the participation of more than 50 partners. AID includes nine research partners, 13+ international researcher collaborators, and more than 50 partners from industry and public sector.

It is built using Svelte + TypeScript, and will be deployed using Netlify.
